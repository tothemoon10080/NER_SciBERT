Concise descriptions of mineral systems that are utilized throughout this book are presented here, except for porphyry Cu–Au–Mo systems and orogenic gold systems, which are presented in detail in Chapter 2. A summary of the mineral systems in terms of their four main components: Geodynamics, Fertility, Architecture, and Preservation, although discussion of Preservation for many systems sited close to the margins of thick mantle lithosphere is reserved for Chapter 9 have been elaborated with the help of table. More exhaustive descriptions of deposit types are provided by textbooks on mineral or ore deposits.
