The Filtration Group remained acquisitive in the second half of 2013, adding Porex and Buffalo to its growing portfolio. Clarcor was also active acquiring both NV Bekaert SA's Bekaert Advanced Filtration and General Electric Co's Power & Water division in the fourth quarter. Other major deals included Ceco buying Met-Pro, SKF acquiring Kaydon and Schweitzer-Mauduit purchasing DelStar Inc.
