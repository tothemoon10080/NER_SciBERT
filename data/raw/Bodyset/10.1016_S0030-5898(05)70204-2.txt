Use of small wire ring fixators in fracture care increased rapidly in North America in the 1990s. Originally modeled on the fixators developed by Ilizarov in the Soviet Union, modern fixators have been altered and have a wide variety of applications. These small wire fixators are commonly referred to as Ilizarov fixators. Differences exist between the application of the Ilizarov technique, the original device, and the small wire fixators available today, however.
