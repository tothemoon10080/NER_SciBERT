 It was the 2nd Physical Separation conference to be held in Falmouth, and was attended by 52 delegates, representing 18 countries. This special issue contains selected papers from the conference. Authors were invited to submit final papers after the event, in order to give them the opportunity of taking into account discussions at the meeting when editing their final work.
