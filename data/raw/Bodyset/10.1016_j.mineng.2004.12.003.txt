The papers in this special issue were presented at Reagents ’04, which was held at the Falmouth Beach Resort Hotel, Falmouth, Cornwall, England from June 16–18, 2004. The event was sponsored by Akzo Nobel and Cytec. Final papers were submitted after the conference, to allow authors to edit their draft papers after conference discussion.
