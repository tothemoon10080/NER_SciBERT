The shoulder joint is more prone to sustaining trauma and the most common injuries to the shoulder include fractures of the proximal humerus, dislocation of glenohumeral and acromioclavicular joints and clavicle fractures. Fractures of the proximal humerus are one of the most frequent injuries involving the appendicular skeleton and account for about 6% of all the extremity injuries.
