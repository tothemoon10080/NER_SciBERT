This Special Issue of Powder Technology comprises peer reviewed papers presented in the Particle Technology Forum sessions at the 2003 Annual AIChE meeting in San Francisco (November, 2003). These papers were peer-reviewed in 2004, modified, and accepted for publication. Papers in this Special Issue cover a wide range of topics related to powder technology including aerosols, comminution, dispersion, agglomeration, granulation, flocculation, energetic materials, and process control. We'd like to thank the authors for submitting these papers and the reviewers for helping us to ensure a quality collection of papers.
