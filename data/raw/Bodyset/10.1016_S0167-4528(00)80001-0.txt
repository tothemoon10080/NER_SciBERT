The history of Mineral Processing describes a course back to an origin rich in symbolism, followed by strong consolidated experiments, and, finally, suitable of refined rational analysis and efficient synthesis of models. The XXI International Mineral Processing Congress (XXI IMPC), following the tradition initiated in 1952, presents the development of scientific knowledge and the most recent industrial applications, to compare know how's of scientists and technicians with the perspectives for the research and for the industrial activity in the sector of Mineral Processing, which now also definitely include topics of waste treatment, recycling and soil remediation.
