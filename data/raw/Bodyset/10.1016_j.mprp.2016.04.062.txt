Höganäs AB has appointed Polypaz, based in Turkey, to cover its market for iron powder and high alloy metal powder in that country. Polypaz has acted as Höganäs’ agent for the polymer and textile filtration industry since almost ten years. Now, its responsibility has expanded to cover also customers in other industries. Polypaz has employed Mehmet Cakici to handle the new area of responsibility. ‘Mehmet has long experience in Metallurgical Engineering and we look forward to our future cooperation,’ said Thierry Calmes, sales director, Höganäs France.
