It is my pleasure to introduce the conference proceedings of the 10th Thailand International Metallurgy Conference, the 10th TIMETC, which contains the selected written version of contributions presented during the conference. The conference is co-organized between Kasetsart University and King Mongkut’s University of Technology North Bangkok to serve as a platform for international as well as domestic researchers, scientists, engineers and technicians in metallurgy and materials science to exchange their new findings and expertise. The theme of the conference is “Innovation through industrial creation” which aims to drive research and innovation towards the 21st century.
