The papers in this special issue were presented at The Hydrocyclone is one of the most widely used separating devices in industry. It would be difficult to find a mineral processing operation in the world which does not use this device, but it also has wide use in many other industries, such as the chemical, oil and gas, pulp and paper, and food industries. Hydrocyclones'03 was a multi-disciplinary conference, where users of this ubiquitous device came together to discuss different approaches to common problems. Final papers were submitted after the conference, to allow authors to edit their draft papers after conference discussion. All papers have been peer-reviewed before publication in this issue.
